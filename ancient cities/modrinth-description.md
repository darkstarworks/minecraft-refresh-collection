# Luki's Ancient Cities

> **A community-refreshed re-release.** Original by lukidonu — now updated & maintained by darkstarworks.

New **Ancient Cities** to get lost in — bigger, more varied, and far more rewarding to explore than the vanilla deep-dark layout. This data pack overhauls Ancient City generation so every city comes out larger, with more custom buildings, bridges and structures instead of the same handful of vanilla rooms — all while the Warden still lurks in the dark below.

> **Pairs perfectly with Better Ancient Cities:** these cities still generate as regular `minecraft:ancient_city` structures, so a management plugin like Better Ancient Cities (AncientCityPro) can discover, snapshot, protect, auto-reset and manage each one — turning it into renewable, multiplayer-friendly content. Find it alongside my other plugins in the [ESMP collection](https://modrinth.com/organization/esmp).

## What's new in v1.3
- Refreshed for Minecraft 26.x
- Natural mob spawns are now suppressed throughout the city — no more mobs spawning in the dark (matching vanilla Ancient Cities)
- Added weathering to the deepslate — some bricks and tiles now generate cracked

## Why this re-release?

The original was created by **[lukidonu](https://modrinth.com/user/lukidonu)**, who has since **discontinued all of their projects** (*"Projects are discontinued #RIP"*). I tried to reach the author but wasn't able to make contact, so I've taken over maintenance to keep the project alive and compatible with current versions of Minecraft.
